Our Vision

NextPointo aims to make world-class sports analytics accessible to everyone. Using just your phone, our AI tracks rallies, movement, and key moments — no sensors, no friction. From squash to tennis, courts to fields, and beyond into computer vision analytics.

Inspiration

Squash taught us resilience: it’s not the last rally that matters, but the next one. That’s the heart of NextPointo — always focusing on the next rally, next checkpoint, next data point. Whatever your sport, we’ll help you see it clearer.
